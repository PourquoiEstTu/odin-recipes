Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

This chicken noodle soup is made with plenty of vegetables, chicken breast, and some noodles to end it all off! The prep time for this will be around 15 minutes while the time to cook is around 45 minutes, giving you around 1 hour's worth of work.

Nutrition Facts

  • Calories: 330 kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 21 g
  • Protein: 35 g
  • Fat: 7 g
  • Fiber: 3 g
  • Sugar: 5 g

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 medium carrots diced (1.5 cups)
  • 4 large celery stalks diced (1 cup)
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion finely diced
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion finely diced
  • 1.5 lbs. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons coarse Italian seasoning*
  • 1 tablespoon Franks hot sauce or white vinegar*
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt or more, to taste
  • 6 sprigs of fresh thyme
  • 6 sprigs of rosemary
  • 3 cups egg noodles*
  • 1 medium lemon sliced
If you don't have coarse Italian seasoning, you can instead use 2 teaspoons normal Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon minced dried garlic, 1 teaspoon minced dried onion, and 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (or to taste).

If you don't like hot sauce, you can use white vinegar instead!

If you want to freeze the soup, keep the noodles separate from the soup and add them to the soup before serving it.

Instructions

  1. Place 2 tablespoons of olive oil on the bottom of a large stock pot.
  2. Then, add in carrots, celery, garlic, and yellow onion and sautee over medium/high heat for 5-7 minutes or until the onions become translucent.
  3. Place chicken breast on top of veggies and add chicken broth, Italian seasoning, hot sauce, and salt. Mix.
  4. Create your herb bundle by tying together fresh thyme and rosemary. Place that into the pot.
  5. Bring soup to a rolling boil over high heat, uncovered.
  6. Once boiling, turn down to low, cover, and let simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. While soup is boiling, prepare egg noodles by bringing a large pot of water to a boil. Add noodles and cook until noodles are al dente. Remove from heat, strain, and set aside.
  8. Once chicken is fully cooked, remove chicken breast and herb bundle from soup.
  9. Discard herb bundle and dice chicken breast into small chunks.
  10. Then, place chicken back into the soup and mix.
  11. Add lemon slices to the pot and let soup simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  12. Finally, add the cooked noodles to the soup right before serving.
